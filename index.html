I am a project leader of the project group “Machine Learning on Graphs” in the Department of Machine Learning and Systems Biology. Before I was a postdoc in the Machine Learning & Computational Biology Lab of Karsten Borgwardt at ETH Zürich.

I obtained my PhD in 2020 from Inria and Université Grenoble-Alpes. During my PhD, I worked in the THOTH team and LBBE lab on machine learning and bioinformatics under supervision of Julien Mairal and Laurent Jacob. I worked on (deep) kernel methods for structured data (sequences, graphs, etc.) and their applications in bioinformatics. I was funded by AMX.

I received my Bachelor and Master degrees from Ecole Polytechnique and another Master degree in applied Maths from ENS Paris Saclay.
